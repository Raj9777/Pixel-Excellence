FAQ

Common questions
answered.

How long does a typical project take? +
A standard website takes 1–2 days (Starter) or 4–6 days (Growth). A custom software project typically takes 30–90 days depending on complexity. We always give you a detailed timeline before starting, and stick to it.
Do you work with startups or established businesses? +
Both! We've built MVPs for Day-1 startups and complex platforms for established companies with thousands of users. Our process adapts to your stage and budget.
What do you need from me to get started? +
Just a clear brief about your goals, target audience, and any design preferences. We'll handle the rest — including content strategy, design, and technical architecture.
Do you provide ongoing support after launch? +
Yes. All projects include a bug-fix period post-launch. We also offer monthly maintenance retainers that include updates, backups, performance monitoring, and priority support.
Can I see examples of your previous work? +
Absolutely! Visit our Portfolio page to see case studies with detailed breakdowns of the challenges we solved and the results we achieved for our clients.
How does payment work? +
We typically work on a 40-40-20 milestone model: 40% to start, 40% at mid-project review, and 20% on final delivery. For smaller projects, we offer 50-50 payment terms.
