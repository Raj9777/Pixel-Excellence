Our Process

How every project works.

01
Discovery Call (Free)
We start with a 30-minute call to understand your business, goals, audience, and budget. No sales pitch — just an honest conversation about what you need and whether we're the right fit.
02
Proposal & Scoping
We deliver a detailed project proposal within 48 hours — including scope, timeline, team, and a fixed quote. We define success metrics upfront so both sides know exactly what winning looks like.
03
Design & Prototype
Our designers build wireframes and high-fidelity Figma prototypes for your review before development starts. You approve every screen — so there are no surprises in the final product.
04
Build & Test
Development happens in weekly sprints. You review working builds at each milestone. Our QA team tests every feature on every device before it's considered complete.
05
Launch & Beyond
We handle deployment, domain setup, and final QA. Then we monitor your product post-launch and provide support. Most clients stay with us for ongoing maintenance, new features, and growth initiatives.
